About Us

Bountiful Foods was founded in 2018 by a group of friends who wanted to make a difference in the world. We believe that everyone should have access to healthy, organic foods. We also believe that everyone should have access to the outdoors. That is why we have partnered with local, organic farms to provide our customers with the freshest ingredients possible. We also have partnered with local parks and recreation departments to provide our customers with a place to enjoy their drinks.

About the Area

Carlsbad is a coastal city in the North County region of San Diego County, California, United States. The city is 87 miles (140 km) south of downtown Los Angeles and 35 miles (56 km) north of downtown San Diego. As of the 2020 census, the population of the city was 114,746. Carlsbad is a popular tourist destination and home to many businesses in the golf industry. It's known for Tamarack Surf Beach, backed by the Carlsbad Sea Wall, and secluded South Carlsbad State Beach. The Legoland California theme park includes rides, a water park and the SEA LIFE Aquarium. The Flower Fields is a seasonal attraction, with acres of flowers. Set in a canyon, the Leo Carrillo Ranch Historic Park is home to free-roaming peacocks. (Source: Wikipedia)
