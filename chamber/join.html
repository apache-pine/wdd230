Join the Yakima Chamber of Commerce

NP Membership Benefits - No Fee

This membership level is designed for non-profit organizations. A package designed to help non-profits raise awareness and get their names out to the public.

Bronze Membership Benefits - $400

Designed for the small start-up or entrepreneur seeking visibility, growth and awareness while making new connections. Get involved, market your business and build relationships.

Silver Membership Benefits - $1000

Establish your business as a community supporter as you grow. Promote multiple branches and services, increasing visibility by showing support of Chamber programs with sponsorship allocation in membership.

Gold Membership Benefits - $2500

Build on your business reputation as a community influencer. You and your business will interact at the highest level of leadership and visibility in the Yakima Chamber. Affect change, establish policy, and contribute to the growth and development of Yakima. Businesses and their executives investing at this top level are widely recognized for their vision and leadership.
