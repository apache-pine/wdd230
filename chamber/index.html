Community Christmas Tree Arrives Nov. 22nd

Yakima's Community Christmas Tree arrived on Tuesday, November 22nd. The Downtown Association of Yakima (DAY) crew secured a tree provided by a Yakima area residence, moved the tree to Millennium Plaza, and placed the tree into the ground on Tuesday.
