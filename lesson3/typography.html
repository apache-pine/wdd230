Typography

Headings will be in the Roboto font. Backup fonts are Arial, followed by sans-serif. Background color is Accent 1 and font color is Primary. See table below for colors. See heading above for an example.

Paragraphs will be in the Oswald font. Backup fonts are Helvetica, followed by sans-serif. Paragraphs will usually be black font on a white background, but occasionally be black font on Accent 3 background or white font on Secondary background. See the Navigation bar above for examples of white on Secondary and black on Accent 3. These paragraphs are in the Oswald font and an example of black text on a white background.
