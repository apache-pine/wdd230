Site Purpose

The purpose of a Chamber of Commerce is to create a community around businesses and local organizations. The Chamber promotes and furthers the interestes of small and local businesses through organizing activities, charitable work, sharing resources, and lobbying local government and representatives.
The purpose of this website is to assist the Chamber if Commerce in achieving these goals. This site provides an online presence for the Chamber, making them more accessible to the public. This site provides a space for the Chamber to post announcements, share contact information, and display the membership application form, among other things.
