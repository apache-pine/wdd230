Persona:

Owner of Yakima Home Loans

Fictional Name:

Michael Nelson

Job Title:

Owner, Office Manager

Demographics:
  • 48 years old
  • Married
  • Father of two children
  • Grandfather of one child
  • 27 years in the industry

Goals and Tasks:

Focused on growing his business and hiring more loan officers. Frequently hosts local events and donates to local charities. Concerned with well being of family and community.

Spends his work time:

  • Filing and reviewing loan applications,
  • preparing reports for office, and
  • supervising staff efforts.

Environment:

He is comfortable using a computer for tasks at wor, but would not refer to himself as being necessarily tech savvy. He uses email and other softwares and applications throughout his work hours, usually about 5-6 hours a day.

Persona 2

Picture:

Persona:

Owner of Yakima Valley Coffee

Fictional Name:

William Matthews

Job Title:

Owner, Branch Manager

Demographics:
  • 32 years old
  • Married
  • Father of three children
  • Has B.A. in Business

Goals and Tasks:

He is comfortable in his position and confident. He wants to open another shop in town and is looking for local investors to expand.

Spends his work time:

  • Networking with potential investors,
  • maintaining inventory, and
  • supervising staff.

Environment:

He is comfortable using a computer and refers to himself as an intermediate Internet user. He frequently uses email and uses the internet about 2 hours during his work day to purchase products for inventory and maintain employee schedules.

Scenarios:

Scenario 1

Micael Nelson visits the site to find a list of local real estate agencies and offices. He is planning an event and wants to send fliers to all the local agencies and offices, but needs a list with contact info and addresses. He is able to navigate to the directory and find what he is looking for.

Scenario 2

Michael Nelson is looking for an event that he and his office can attend to be out in public and hand out cards and fliers. He wants his loan officers to have the chance to meet people in the community and grow their own businesses and networks. He looks over the home page, searching for information about upcoming events.

Scenario 3

William Matthews wants to expand his business and open another coffee shop in town. He is a local business owner and doesn't operate a chain, so he is looking for local investors. He wants to network with others who would be willing to invest. He looks at the join page in an attempt to decide which membership option would be best for him and his needs.

Scenario 4

Michael Nelson wants to improve his advertisments locally as well as online and on social media. He needs to know which advertisments would be most beneficial in which places. He goes to the discover portion of the website to find information on the current demographics of the city to better target his ads and marketing.

Scenario 5

William Matthews still can't decide which membership option is best for him. He decides to look at the home page to learn about any upcoming events that the Chamber is hosting so he might attend and learn more. He also finds his way to the contact page and submits a contact form and looks through the options to learn more.
